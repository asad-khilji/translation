Surah Al Fatihah

The Opening

Revealed in Makkah

With the name of Allah, the extremely loving & caring right now, the always loving & caring

1. All praise and thanks is for Allah, Master of all nations and people.

2. The extremely loving & caring right now, the always loving & caring.

3. Master of the day of judgement.

4. You alone do we willingly enslave ourselves to, and You alone do we desperately seek help from.

5. Guide us along the straight path, which is upwards.

6. The path of those whom You have favored.

7. But not the path of those who have wrath upon themselves, nor those who are lost.

Ameen
